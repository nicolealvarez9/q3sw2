MOVIE LIST
YOUR NAME (KIMI NO NA WA)
Your Name is a Japanese film centered around the story of a young girl from the countryside named Mitsuha and a young city boy named Taki. Throughout the movie, they find that they swap bodies each time they sleep. This situation soon gets more complicated than just trying to live each other's lives as their connection is both magical and spiritual after all. Release date: December 16, 2014
LITTLE WOMEN
Based on the classic novel of Louisa May Alcott, we are taken through the lives of four sisters: Meg, Jo, Amy, and Beth March during the American Civil War. The film tells the stories of these four girls by showing us both their present and past alternately. Release date: December 25, 2019
THE SECRET WORLD OF ARRIETTY
Arrietty is a gentle but daring little girl. She is quite literally little in size, being as tall as a small leaf. She and her family live secretly and peacefully underneath the home of two humans until a young boy struggling with health conditions comes to live in the house to get rest before his heart operation. Release date: July 17, 2010
MAMMA MIA! HERE WE GO AGAIN
In this sequel, we are taken through a big part of the life of Donna Sheridan before she had her daughter Sophie. As this happens, we also see Sophie's own present journey of opening the hotel her mother once dreamed of managing. Release date: July 20, 2018
AVATAR: THE WAY OF WATER
This successful sequel to one of the top-grossing movies of all time once again follows the story of characters Jake Sully and Neytiri, now along with their children, as they do all they can to keep their family and people safe for another time. Release date: December 16, 2022
